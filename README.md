## 📖 User Guide: Features & How to Use Them

This app is packed with advanced features designed to make managing your finances as frictionless as possible. Here is a breakdown of what it can do and how to use it:

### 📚 1. Organize with Workspaces ("Books")
**How it works:** Instead of dumping all your expenses into one massive list, you can separate your finances into isolated "Books". 
**How to use:**
* Open the app and tap the **Book Name** at the very top of the Home Screen.
* Tap the `+` icon to create a new Book (e.g., "Personal Finances 2026" or "Bali Trip").
* You can switch between Books at any time. When a book is no longer needed, swipe to archive it so it doesn't clutter your view!

### ✍️ 2. Adding Expenses & Sub-Expenses
**How it works:** The Add Expense sheet lets you input large expenses, or break them down into granular items (sub-expenses) with individual quantities.
**How to use:**
* Tap the massive `+` button on the Home Screen.
* Enter the total amount, or skip the total and start adding **Sub-Expenses** below.
* For each sub-expense (e.g., "Milk"), you can set a specific quantity using the capsule selector, and the app will mathematically calculate your grand total for you!
* Select a category and hit Save.

### 📸 3. Smart Receipt Scanner (OCR) - *Experimental*
**How it works:** Built using advanced on-device Text Recognition, the app can read standard receipts and automatically convert them into Expense Reports. *(Note: This feature is currently experimental and accuracy may vary depending on lighting and receipt format).*
**How to use:**
* Take a photo of a receipt using your phone's camera.
* Share that photo from your gallery directly to the **AR Expense Tracker** app.
* The app will open, scan the image locally, identify the line items, and instantly fill out the Add Expense sheet for you!

### 📊 4. Insights & Budgeting
**How it works:** Get a visual breakdown of exactly where your money is going with dynamic pie charts and custom category budgets.
**How to use:**
* Tap the **Insights** icon on the bottom navigation bar.
* View your beautifully animated pie chart to see your highest spending categories.
* Tap **Manage Budgets** to assign a maximum spending limit to a specific category (e.g., "Limit 'Food' to ₹5,000").
* The Insights screen will visually show you how close you are to hitting your limit.

### 🔄 5. Automate Recurring Expenses
**How it works:** If you pay rent or a Netflix subscription every month, the app can automatically add that expense for you on a set schedule.
**How to use:**
* Go to **Settings > Manage Recurring Expenses**.
* Add a new recurring bill, set the amount, and choose the frequency (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly).
* The app will seamlessly inject this expense into your active Book exactly when it's due.

### ☁️ 6. 100% Free Cloud Sync
**How it works:** Never lose your financial data. The app connects to your personal Google Drive to safely backup your SQLite database.
**How to use:**
* Go to **Settings** and tap the **Sync** button.
* Sign in with your Google account.
* The app will automatically sync your data to the cloud in the background whenever you make changes. You can even see exactly how much Drive space the app is consuming!

### 📲 7. Zero-Friction In-App Updater
**How it works:** You don't need an App Store to get the latest features. The app talks directly to GitHub to check for updates.
**How to use:**
* Whenever a new release is pushed to GitHub, simply open the app.
* A dialog box will automatically appear asking if you want to install the new update.
* Tap "Update Now" to be taken directly to the latest download!
